Panda Home Healthcare INC

Panda home healthcare is committed to providing quality service with our experienced and compassionate professionals delivering in home health care for the ill, disabled, elderly and homebound.

Skilled Nursing

The registered Nurse and Licensed Vocational Nurses assess your physical condition, communicate with your doctor, and provide necessary treatment. Skilled nursing services may include but not limited to:

  • Catheter Care
  • Diabetic Care
  • Pre-op and post-op Care
  • Wound Care
  • IV Therapy
  • Ostomy Care
  • Respiratory Care
  • Family/Patient Education

Certified Home Health Aides

Home health Aides will assist you in personal care, meal preparation. Ambulation and exercise under professional supervision.

Social Services

Deals with social and emotional factors related to illness. Our social workers provide therapeutic assistance that helps our patients recover and recuperate faster.

Dietary Consultation

Nutritional counseling to help incorporate your prescribed diet into your lifestyle.

Physical, Occupational, Speech Therapy

Assist in improving patents’ ability to perform daily activities. Our therapists implement appropriate assistance in regaining strength, coordination, regaining function, compensatory techniques, and speech problems.

Durable Medical Equipment.

Arrangements for durable medical equipment will be made to meet the needs of the patient.

FAQ

Our business hours?

Mon - Friday: 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM
On-call nurse available after office hours,on weekends, and on holidays

Who will need Home Health Care?

  • Chronically ill, terminally ill. or permanently disabled individuals who wish to remain home
  • Patients who need short-term assistance after being discharged from the hospital
  • Elderly/older adults who are ill or disabled and need assistance in their home

Why pays for home health care services?

  • Medicare /Medi-Cal:If you are eligible for home health coverage through Medicare and medical, our office will bill them directly.
  • Direct payment:Payment billed directly to you will include visits and hours spent by home health care professionals. Payment is per visit hour rate.
  • Private and commercial Insurance:We will verify receipt of any assignment of health benefits.
